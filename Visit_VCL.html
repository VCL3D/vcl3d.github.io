Visit VCL

Location

Visual Computing Lab (VCL) of the Center for Research & Technology Hellas (CERTH) is located 12 km from the centre of the city of Thessaloniki and 8 km from the airport.

How to reach CERTH

from downtown 

  • By bus
  • By car
  • By taxi
  • CERTH on GPS

From the bus stations alongside Egnatia avenue, take the bus No.10 [Charilaou – New Railway Station] towards Charilaou.
At the Bus Terminal Charilaou switch to Bus No.66 [Charilaou – Thermi].Get off at the bus station “Zoni Kenotomias” in front of the CERTH premises.

Duration: approx. 45 -60 min
Cost: The two sequential directions ticket costs €1, 30. For students and seniors the ticket costs €0,70. Ticket machines are located on all buses but they do not give out change. A cheaper alternative (€1,20/€0,60) is to purchase your ticket from the ticket booths at the public bus central stations or at some of the kiosks located across the main streets.
Relevant information can be found on the OASTH (Urban Transport Organization of Thessaloniki) website.

Once you take the Ring Road from the vicinity of the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki, drive on heading to the south and reach exit 12 to Chalkidiki, after about 2 kilometres, leave the Highway at the exit Foinikas/Thermi and then turn right.

Taxis in Thessaloniki are blue colored. Meters are set at the start of each route with a minimum fare applying of €3, 20.

Duration: approx. 20 - 30 min
Cost: approx. €10 - €15
